About Me

Greetings! Thank you for taking the time to read a little bit about me. I grew up in Washington state; a little town called Spanaway to be exact, and I shared a home with my parents and older sister. As a child I was always creating something whether it be turmoil between my sister and I, or projects such as home movies and musical pieces on the ever timeless recorder. Physical activities became much more prominent for me as I grew older and I fell in love with soccer and combat sports. For a few years I was the lead singer/rhythm guitar player for a metal band called Lady Luck.
I currently reside in Tacoma, Washington working in beverage distribution as a truck driver for a company called NW Beverages. Most of my free time consists of playing soccer, video games, and polishing my skills in the gym, whether that be with weights or a heavy bag. Of course that kid inside of me still lingers around and my creative juices never stop flowing. In my never ending quest to constantly create and solve, I joined a UW coding bootcamp where I am currently building my skills as a full stack web developer. As it stands now, taking my first steps into the world of programming has been a huge commitment; and a very daunting one that I've been putting off for far too long. So far I'm absolutely in love with everything I'm learning and I hope to continue growing my knowledge, and eventually grow into a career in programming.
